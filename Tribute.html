Keanu Reeves

This is my tribute page to one of the greatest actors!

Keanu Reeves has had an extensive career spanning over 34 years

Keanu was best known for his roles in The Matrix Trilogy but there are many more films he has been cast in. One film in particular that I loved growing up was Point Break. In this film he played a federal agent who was infiltrating a group of bank robbers lead by a surfer named Bodhi. Bodhi was played by another well known actor, the late Patrick Swayze.

Some of the films Keanu Reeves has been in are:

  • The Matrix Trilogy
  • Constantine
  • Speed
  • The Devil’s Advocate
  • Bill & Ted‘s Excellent Adventure
  • John Wick 1&2

John Wick 3 will be released sometime in May 2019 which myself and my husband can't wait to see!
